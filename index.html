About

I am Natural Language Processing Master's student at University of California, Santa Cruz (Silicon Valley Campus).

Prior to my move to California, I worked as Senior Machine Learning Engineer at Quantiphi Analytics Solutions in Mumbai, India. Quantiphi is a analytics based consultation firm where I worked on building and prototyping various Machine learning and Deep Learning solutions.

In my personal time, I like to spend time with my friends, explore the Santa Clara and San Jose town, go on hikes and paint abstract art using acrylic medium.
